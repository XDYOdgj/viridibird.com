Probrico Flat Black Passage Door Lever

Enhance the security and aesthetics of your home's exterior with the Probrico Flat Black Passage Door Lever. This upgrade combines a stylish, lockless design with a high-performance single-cylinder deadbolt to deliver a modern and seamless door lock solution. Ideal for doors up to 1-3/4 inches thick, it features an adjustable backset for easy and secure installation.

Inside, the thumb-turn button allows for quick and convenient locking from within, while the matching key on the outside ensures effortless unlocking. With 12 set keys included, this set is perfect for both residential and commercial use, offering reliable protection and a touch of sophistication to your doorway.

Backed by ANSI Grade 3 verification, the Probrico Door Lever Set offers unmatched strength, durability, and performance. Whether you're looking to upgrade your home or apartment security system, this lockless door lever and deadbolt set bring convenience, control, and peace of mind to every entry.
